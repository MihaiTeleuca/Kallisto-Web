00. Executive Preamble

Welcome to the Kallisto Master Privacy Protocol. Kallisto operates far beyond the scope of traditional Discord applications; it is a highly advanced ecosystem utilizing dynamic database clustering, asynchronous process execution, and micro-transaction simulation algorithms.

Because our framework processes enterprise-grade ticketing systems, global economy simulations, and absolute moderation logs, our commitment to Data Obfuscation, User Anonymity, and Cryptographic Security is absolute. This document outlines the extreme measures deployed by our infrastructure to guarantee compliance with the highest international standards.

Zero-Knowledge Content Promise Kallisto employs a strict "Zero-Knowledge" architecture regarding standard text communication. The Bot does not log, read, or process raw message content within your server unless a command is explicitly invoked or a ticket channel is actively archiving. We do not listen to your conversations.

01. Algorithmic Data Ingestion

To ensure sub-millisecond execution accuracy across thousands of synchronized Guilds, Kallisto ingests strictly necessary telemetry directly from the authenticated Discord API Gateway. We categorize this ingestion into two core vectors:

A. Ephemeral Routing Telemetry (Cache)

During the execution of a Slash Command (/interaction) or a button press, Kallisto temporarily holds routing data in active memory (RAM) to deliver the payload. This data is purged instantly after the interaction concludes.

  • 64-bit Snowflake Identifiers: The absolute mathematical ID assigned to your Discord profile.
  • Cryptographic Hashes: Avatar and Banner hashes used exclusively to render embed interfaces.
  • Role Bitfields: Binary data confirming your permission level (e.g., Administrator vs. User) to block unauthorized execution of moderation modules.

B. Persistent Architecture Modules

When Server Administrators deploy our persistent modules (Economy, Tickets, Leveling), specific metrics are securely written to our encrypted database clusters to maintain state across reboots.

03. Cryptographic Storage & Time-To-Live (TTL)

Data at rest and data in transit are handled using military-grade enterprise standards. We refuse to compromise on structural security.

▶ Inspect Cryptographic Standards (AES-256 & TLS 1.3)

All communication between the Discord API and the Kallisto central servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Persistent database clusters are encrypted at rest using the AES-256 encryption algorithm. Direct access to the database layer is blocked via strict IP whitelisting and firewall rules, rendering unauthorized external queries impossible.

▶ Inspect Time-To-Live (TTL) & Cache Pruning

Kallisto operates an aggressive pruning algorithm. Ephemeral memory states (used for paginating embeds or tracking short-term cooldowns) are aggressively flushed every 15 minutes. Persistent data (Economy/Levels) relies on user sovereignty—meaning it stays safe until you explicitly command its deletion.

04. Infrastructure Isolation & Third Parties

The Kallisto architecture operates in a strictly isolated vacuum within the Discord ecosystem.

  • Zero Corporate Monetization: Your data is not a commodity. We do not sell, lease, transfer, or exchange our database shards with marketing conglomerates, data brokers, or advertising networks.
  • Cross-Guild Isolation: Data generated in Server A is mathematically isolated from Server B. A server administrator from one community cannot query, extract, or view the moderation logs, ticket histories, or exact balances of a user in a different community.

05. Digital Sovereignty (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Regardless of your geographic location, we grant all users the maximum rights mandated by the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Article 17: The Right to Erasure You maintain absolute sovereignty over your digital footprint. At any given moment, you may trigger the "Right to be Forgotten" protocol. Submitting a verified request will initiate a cascade deletion, mathematically wiping your User ID, economy balances, items, and levels from all Kallisto database clusters globally. Note: This process is irreversible.

To execute a data request or a complete architectural purge, contact the lead developer natively via Discord or open a high-priority ticket in the Kallisto Support HQ. Purges are verified and executed within 72 hours.

06. Incident & Breach Protocols

In the highly unlikely event of a catastrophic infrastructure breach or unauthorized database access, Kallisto maintains an automated response protocol. The affected database shards will be instantly isolated from the main network. Furthermore, a global broadcast will be issued to all registered Server Owners within 24 hours of breach verification, detailing the extent of the compromised vector.
